MY STORY


See Resume

I first discovered my passion for UX design, when I started a relocation blog years ago after living in 5 cities over 6 years. At the time, I thought I was just spending hours playing around with the layout to make it more user friendly, but later learned that I was actually programming in CSS! I was enjoying myself so much that I decided to leave my career as an established physical therapist and pursue my passion for UX/UI design and I have never looked back

Before I knew it, I got my Certification for Full Stack Web Development (Coding Bootcamp) from University of Texas Austin and my UX Design certification with a specialization in UX for Voice UI from Careerfoundry. My work experience as a Solution Engineer with UI/UX focus at ORACLE just further confirmed my passion for UX design. Currently, I work as a UX/UI designer at a startup and I also tutor UX design at Careerfoundry.
