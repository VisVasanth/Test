HB HOMES
UX PROCESS FOR CHATBOTS


The HomeBuilder Homes engagement was to create 2 web-based chatbots that would save time for HB Homes' employees as well as a potential homebuyers. HB Homes is a home construction company that allows home buyers to build their homes with ease.

Process Overview

After multiple ideation sessions, I began to brainstorm on personas for the 2 storyboards (one for each use case). This allowed me to add emotion to the use case and create a meaningful storyboard. After doing some perusing on the client site, I then brainstormed the questions appropriate for the chatbots and used those to create high fidelity wireframes.

DELIVERABLES:

Stakeholder Interviews, Proto-Personas, Storyboards, Wireframes, Programmed Prototype (HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node, Oracle IBCS)

Step 1: Define

Future Home Buyer Chatbot

Business Impact for Use Case 1

This chatbot allows future home buyers to inquire and select their dream home to be built. In addition, this allows the HB Home's Sales Agents and New Home Reps to work more efficiently because the chatbot will walk the home buyer through the basic questions. So when the home buyer is assigned to a Sales Agents or New Home Rep, they will already have the home buyer's information and they will only get assigned to serious buyers.

Lessons Learned

While the whole project was a huge learning experience, I especially loved sitting with the client through an ideation phase with multiple meetings. This allowed me to really understand the purpose behind the chatbots we were building and how it would benefit HB Homes and the end user. Creating personas for my storyboard allowed me to solidify the use cases from the ideation session with the clients. This allowed me to design wireframes in Sketch with a more meaningful purpose, which was well recieved by the client.
